The Minds Behind Korroboree



Lina Ashar Co-Founder Korroboree

A teacher at heart, Lina Ashar has always been super enthusiastic when it comes to crafting a model of education that is fulfilling, effective and yet stress-free. With her primary education completed in England, Lina moved to Australia, where she acquired a bachelor’s degree in Education from Victoria College in Melbourne. In 1991, when Lina came on a year long sabbatical from college, the Indian education system appalled her.

Let’s Create The Best Version Of Ourselves

Ensuring a better future for the next generation through maximising their potential



AWARENESS Access your potential

Most of us are never fully aware of our potential. From a very young age, we are told what we can do and what we cannot. When curious kids question us or ask way too many why’s, we do need their curiosity.
Read more

MIND Understand your potential

The beauty and the importance of mindful parenting lies in raising children who behave well. Instead of reminding our children that they need to excel in the pre-defined educational system, we need to inculcate positive thinking to help them become “anti-fragile”.
Read more

HEART Feel your potential

Often, as parents, we stress more on success rather than effort. In case, a child secures marks below the predefined average in an examination, we are quick to judge him as someone with a mediocre future. As soon as we tag a child as unsuccessful because of his low IQ, we immediately set a limit to his potential.
Read more

SOUL Discover your potential

While the current education system still focuses more on textbooks and examinations, a shift in parenting from ‘protective upbringing’ to ‘purposeful upbringing’ can do wonders for the little ones. To uplift their spirits and nurture their souls, let their curiosity grow.
Read more

INNER WORK Maximise your potential

With Korroboree explore and embrace mindful parenting. We offer resources where you can take a glimpse of how to become your child’s coach instead of being their guide. We just don’t tell you how to raise your child; we take you on a journey where you witness the benefits of discovering your child’s
Read more

ATTRIBUTES Embrace your potential

Parents need to shift their roles from managing their child to becoming their coaches. To establish a culture of learning, innovation and creativity, and exploring their full potential, parents need to be goal setters and guide their children as consultants through varied experiences.
